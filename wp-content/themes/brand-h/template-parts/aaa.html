Who is IVF recommended for? 
Patients who have :
· Experienced multiple failures in insemination in the past
· Abnormal female reproductive system
· Immunological infertility with certain antibodies
· Other types of unexplained infertility for which other treatment methods have all failed
Who is ICSI recommended for?
For Male patients with :
· A low sperm count and or poor sperm quality
· Obstructive or non-obstructive azoospermia that cannot be resolved with surgical correction
· Ejaculation disorder caused by spinal cord injury or other urethral obstruction
Who would benefit from Embryo Glue? Are there any risks to using Embryo Glue?
Although embryo glue is available to any patient, couples who have had multiple cycles without a successful implantation may benefit the most.
